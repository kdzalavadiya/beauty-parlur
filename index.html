Your Wedding Journey

A step-by-step guide to your perfect bridal experience

3-6 Months Before

Initial Consultation

Schedule your initial consultation to discuss your vision, preferences, and wedding details. We'll help you choose the perfect bridal look and create a personalized beauty plan.

2-3 Months Before

Makeup & Hair Trial

Experience your wedding look beforehand with our detailed trial session. We'll perfect your makeup palette, hairstyle, and accessories to ensure everything is exactly as you envision.

1 Month Before

Pre-Bridal Skincare

Begin your pre-bridal skincare routine to ensure glowing skin on your wedding day. Our specialists will recommend treatments and products tailored to your skin type.

1-2 Days Before

Mehndi Ceremony

Experience the traditional mehndi ceremony with our expert artists creating intricate designs. Allow proper time for the henna to develop into its rich color.

Wedding Day

Bridal Transformation

Relax and enjoy as our team expertly creates your perfect bridal look. We'll handle everything from makeup and hair to outfit draping and jewelry styling.
